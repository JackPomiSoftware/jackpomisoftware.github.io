Contact information

For any software or game-related questions please contact the following e-mail: jackpomisoftware@gmail.com

Please consider the following guidelines before sending:

What you can send?

  • Any feedback, suggestion, bug report about any Jack Pomi Software product (game or software).

  • Technical support request related to any of Jack Pomi Software products.

  • General questions related to Jack Pomi Software or/and its products.

What you should not send?

  • Unsolicited materials - Jack Pomi Software does not accept unsolicited materials of any kind, including but not limited to product-related materials. Unsolicited materials sent via email will be deleted upon receipt.

  • Questions regarding unannounced information about things like release dates or future products.

  • Request for assets.

  • Something completely unrelated to Jack Pomi Software and its products.
